Q&A about the use of technology to help marine pollution

  • How can we use the internet and social media to prevent plastic pollution in the ocean?

Using the power of the internet and social media can help connect people and raise awareness about plastic pollution. There are multiple ways this can be done such as creating websites focussed on the harm of marine pollution and what can be done to prevent the issue. Or even starting a hashtag for example #StopPlasticPollution, this can lead to social media awareness allowing fundraising across the globe.

  • Can advanced technology and machine learning be used to clean the ocean?

Using advanced technology such as satellite imaging and machine learning we can capture the trillions of pieces of plastic and rapidly clean the ocean efficiently. Satellites can be used to detect and track the large quantity of plastic and locate where it is most concentrated to prioritise cleaning up those areas.

  • Can we use artificial intelligence to predict climate patterns due to plastic pollution?

With the use of AI algorithms, we can understand climate change by predicting climate patterns which will allow for better coordination when analysing data on pollution. If we can model and predict future climate patterns, this can allow us to better prepare by planning and creating solutions ahead of time.

  • Will data visualization about marine pollution cause people to act?

Using machine learning, we can gather and visualise data about plastic in the ocean.   • Can blockchain technology be used to effectively reduce plastic waste?

A bank could be created offering blockchain secured digital tokens in exchange for recycled plastics. This could drastically reduce pollution by rewarding those who choose to recycle.
